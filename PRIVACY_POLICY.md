# Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the TG-Opinion-Scrapper bot ("Bot", "we", "us") collects, uses, and stores information when used in Telegram groups and chats.

## 1. Information We Collect

The Bot may process:

- Telegram user identifiers (for example, user ID and username)
- Message content sent in the configured group
- Message metadata (for example, message ID, chat ID, timestamp, message link)
- Admin command inputs

If enabled by the operator, the Bot may also process exported Telegram history files for backfilling memory and opinion logs.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

- Detect and forward opinions/ideas to the admin
- Moderate group activity (anti-flood, mute/ban actions)
- Answer `/ask` questions using saved memory and configured knowledge
- Maintain logs and memory files for bot functionality

## 3. Storage and Retention

By default, data is stored locally by the Bot operator in files such as:

- `opinions.jsonl`
- `memory/chat_memory.jsonl`

Data retention is controlled by the Bot operator. We do not automatically delete stored records unless configured by the operator.

## 4. Sharing of Information

The Bot does not sell personal data.

Information may be shared with:

- The configured bot admin (for opinion alerts and moderation handling)
- Service providers used by the operator (for example, hosting providers)
- OpenAI API, if enabled, for generating `/ask` responses

## 5. Legal Basis and Consent

By interacting with the Bot in Telegram, users acknowledge that their messages may be processed for moderation, opinion extraction, and assistant features described in this policy.

## 6. Security

Reasonable technical measures should be used by the Bot operator to protect stored data. However, no method of storage or transmission is fully secure.

## 7. International Processing

Data may be processed and stored in countries different from a user's location, depending on where the Bot is hosted and which external services are enabled.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, users may have rights to access, correction, deletion, or objection to processing. Requests should be directed to the Bot operator/admin.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for children under the age required by applicable local law without parental or guardian consent.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates are effective when published with a revised effective date.

## 11. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact the Bot operator/admin.
